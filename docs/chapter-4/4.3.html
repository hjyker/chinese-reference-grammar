4.3-1 stative verb

4.19.a

stative verbs: 有 you3 ‘have’, 姓 xing4 ‘have the surname of’, 像 xiang4 ‘resemble’, 喜欢 xi3huan1 ‘like’

4.3-2 activity verb

4.19.b

activity verbs: 做 zuo4 ‘make’, 劝 quan4 ‘(try to) persuade/advise’, 吃 chi1 ‘eat’, 打 da3 ‘beat’, 念 nian4 ‘read’,看 kan4 ‘look, read’

4.3-3 achievement verb

4.19.c

achievement verbs: 到 dao4 ‘arrive’, 忘 wang4 ‘forget’, 丢 diu1 ‘lose’, 死亡 si3wang2 ‘die’

4.3-4 accomplishment verb

4.19.d

accomplishment verbs: 毙 bi4 ‘shoot and kill’, 灭 mie4 ‘extinguish’, 娶 qu3 ‘marry (a woman)’, 绝杀 jue2sha1 ‘absolutely kill’, 劝服 quan4fu2 ‘persuade’, 杀死 sha1si3 ‘kill’, 打破 da3po4 ‘break’, 压倒 ya1dao3 ‘crash’
